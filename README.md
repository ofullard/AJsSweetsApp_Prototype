## 📱 Project Overview

This repository contains design assets, screenshots, and documentation for a mobile application prototype designed for AJ's Sweets, a fictional bakery based in Stone Mountain, Georgia. The prototype demonstrates a complete user experience flow for customers to browse products, place orders, and interact with the bakery through a mobile interface.

### Project Type
- **Design Tool**: Figma
- **Project Type**: UI/UX Prototype
- **Platform**: Mobile Application
- **Status**: Design Prototype

At AJ's Sweets, we believe that every bite should be a delightful experience. Our bakery is dedicated to crafting the finest pastries, cakes, and confections that bring joy and sweetness to your life. 
Located in the heart of Stone Mountain, we pride ourselves on using only the highest quality ingredients and traditional baking techniques to create treats that are as beautiful as they are delicious.

### Our Story

AJ's Sweets was founded by Alice Johnson in 2008, with a passion for baking and a dream to share that passion with the community. 
What started as a small home kitchen operation has grown into a beloved local bakery, known for our warm atmosphere and delectable creations. 
From our signature cupcakes to our artisanal breads, every item is made with love and care.

### Our Mission

"To bake gourmet goodies that inspire joy, foster community, and enrich the life of every customer with warmth and sweetness."
